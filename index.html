Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

The founding father of the Republic of Turkey

The time line of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk:

  • 1881 - Born in Salonica
  • 1905 - He graduated from the Army War College as Captain
  • 1905–1907 - Staff captain at Fifth Army
  • 1907–1910 - Senior captain at Third Army
  • 1910-1911 - He entered service at the Ministry of War.
  • 1911-1912 - Later in 1911, he volunteered to fight in the Italo-Turkish war.
  • 1912–1913 - Atatürk fought against the Bulgarian army at First Balkan war.
  • 1914-1918 - WW1
  • 19 May 1919 - Kemal lands in Samsun. Starting of Indepence war⁽¹⁾
  • 23 April 1920 - He opens the Grand National Assembly in Ankara.
  • 1921 - Kemal receives the rank of Marshal and the title Gazi(veteran, victorious fighter).
  • 29 Oct 1923 - Proclamation of the Republic of Turkey and he elected by the people as the first president.
  • 1934 - The Grand National Assembly of Turkey gives him the surname "ATATÜRK".
  • 10 November 1938 - dies at the age of 57.
